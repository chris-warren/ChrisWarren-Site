FireWood

Firewood is a physics-based platformer/puzzle game about a child who, while bringing home wood, goofes off with a magical bird in the woods and looses track of time. The bird, feeling guilty that the child will be home late, gives them a magical gift that allows them to push the wood through the air with their mind.
